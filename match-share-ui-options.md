# Match Share UI Options

1. Always-Visible Icon Pill
- Place a small share/download pill in the top-right of each match card.
- Subtle background, low opacity on desktop, full opacity on mobile.
- Keeps discoverability without clutter.

2. Inline “Share” Row in Footer
- Add a small row below the score/date with a Share/Download label and icon.
- Aligns with the content flow and is easy to tap on mobile.

3. Floating Share Strip
- Add a slim floating bar at the bottom of the Matches page with “Share Latest Match” and a selector.
- Very visible but more intrusive.

4. Tap-to-Reveal on Mobile
- First tap on a card reveals a small share action row; second tap still opens team filter.
- Good for reducing visual noise, but tap behavior can be ambiguous.

5. Left Edge Share Notch
- A small “notch” tab on the right edge of each card with a share icon; always visible.
- Visual affordance without overt buttons.
