# Desktop Share UI Notification Options

This document outlines the proposed UI solutions for notifying desktop users that an image has been downloaded and providing tips for direct sharing.

## Option 1: The "Kinetic Toast" - [SELECTED]
A high-contrast notification bar that slides in from the bottom-right corner of the screen after the download completes.
- **Visuals:** Navy Blue (`brand-blue`) background, bold white text, and a Yellow (`brand-yellow`) accent.
- **Pros:** Modern, non-disruptive, works globally across all sharing triggers.
- **Content:** "Image Downloaded! Check your downloads folder. To share directly, open this site on mobile."

## Option 2: The "Inline Action Message"
Replaces the instructional text in the share section with a success message.
- **Visuals:** Contextual text transition from gray to bold navy/green.
- **Pros:** Very contextual; feels integrated into the specific share section.

## Option 3: The "Post-Action Modal"
A small, centered dialog that appears over the screen.
- **Visuals:** "Warm Paper" texture with a hard shadow.
- **Pros:** High visibility; ensures the message is read.
