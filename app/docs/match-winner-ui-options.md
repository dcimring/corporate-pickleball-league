# Match Winner UI Options

Visual directions for highlighting match winners in the Corporate Pickleball League "Roost Kinetic" theme.

## Option 1: "The Heavyweight Stamp" (Brutalist & High-Impact)
- **Winner Badge**: A bold, tilted "WINNER" watermark in a semi-transparent `brand-yellow` (#FFC72C), styled like a rubber ink stamp, partially overlapping the winning team's name.
- **Shadow Play**: The winning score gains a massive, 3D-style "hard shadow" (4px 4px 0px brand-navy) that makes it feel like it’s punching through the paper.
- **Border Accent**: A thick, 4px vertical "power bar" in `brand-yellow` appears only on the winning team's side of the card.

## Option 2: "Kinetic Energy" (Dynamic & Interactive)
- **Brush Stroke**: A hand-drawn, "rough-edge" highlighter stroke in `brand-yellow` appears behind the winning team's name, making it pop off the page.
- **Victory Marker**: A small, stylized lightning bolt or star icon in `brand-yellow` is placed next to the winning team.
- **Pulse Effect**: The winning team's name and score have a subtle, slow-breathing `scale(1.02)` animation.

## Option 3: "Editorial Prestige" (Refined & Journalistic)
- **The "W" Marker**: A large, elegant serif 'W' in a small circle next to the winner's name.
- **Victory Underline**: The winning team name is underlined with a thick, "ink-bleed" style line.
- **Negative Space Shift**: The winning team's name is given extra letter-spacing (tracking) for a "grand" feel.
