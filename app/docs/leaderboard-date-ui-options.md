## NEW STYLE VARIATIONS (Current Selection)

### Style 4: The "Torn Ledger" (Tactile & Analogue)
- **Visual**: A light `brand-gray` strip with a "perforated" bottom edge and a subtle dot-grid texture.
- **Typography**: `brand-blue` Monospace, tracking-widest.
- **Vibe**: Physical, archival, organic.

### Style 5: The "Recessed Scoreboard" (Mechanical & Inset)
- **Visual**: A deep `brand-blue` bar with an inner shadow to look like a cutout in the UI "paper."
- **Typography**: `brand-yellow` Monospace with a subtle text-glow.
- **Vibe**: High-tech retro, authoritative, mechanical.

### Style 6: The "Editorial Rule" (Refined & Minimalist)
- **Visual**: Transparent background. Text is framed by two thin horizontal `brand-blue` lines.
- **Typography**: `brand-blue` Raleway ExtraBold Italic (Header style).
- **Vibe**: Clean, editorial, sophisticated.
